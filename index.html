Drag cards between columns or reorder within the same column. The board listens once at the board level for dragstart, dragover, drop, and dragend. During dragover, it calculates the nearest insertion point by comparing the mouse Y position against each card’s midpoint with getBoundingClientRect(). On drop, it moves the dragged node with insertBefore().
